Merchandising Division

Merchandising is a business on marketing activities responsible for ensuring product description both quality and quantity. Merchandising means total responsibility of a Merchandiser. A group of 20 merchandisers are working relentlessly and acting as strong bondage in between the buyer and the factory. Merchandising sub‐groups are re‐created to give effective support to the respective buyer to ship goods on time. With sourcing offices with designers and factories to give effective support to respective buyers. Yes Green Fashion Ltd has its Own Head offices in Dhaka port which the gives privilege of quick clearing and forwarding and shipping all our goods on time.
